Coding for a Change provides financial assistance for medical treatment, medication, and quality-of-life experiences for individuals and families affected by the PSEN1 gene mutation.

About Us

A fund built on compassion

Coding for a Change is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Utah. We exist for one reason: to help people carrying the PSEN1 mutation live fuller lives.

The PSEN1 gene mutation is linked to early-onset Alzheimer's disease. Families affected by this condition often face enormous financial and emotional burdens. We believe no one should have to face that alone.

Our fund helps cover the cost of medicine, medical care, and meaningful experiences for patients and their families. Both donors and recipients may remain fully anonymous.
